88rising is an American music company, record label, video production and marketing company. it was founded in 015 by sean miyashiro ans jaeson ma. the company first started as a music collective and management company called CXSHXNLY (cash only) which oversees and began to make contact with up-and-coming artists that they found on the internet. their goal of this company is 'to become the most wavy, iconic crew' and they are trying to represent asian immigrants, but also for all immigrants.

in may 2016 they changed their name to 88rising. they named it '88rising', because the number '8' is a lucky number in china and two eights will stand for dubble luck and happiness.

there are now (2023) seventeen artists under 88rising. which consist of atarashii gakko, bibi, chungha, dumbfoundead, guapdad 40, higherbrothers, jackson wang, joji, keith ape, lexie liu, milli, niki, rich brian, seori, stephanie poetri, warren hue and ylona garcia.
